Game Rules

  • 20x20 board
  • Aim to place 21 pieces
  • Each player could control one or two colours
  • Initial move is to place pieces on the board corners
  • Subsequent turn must involve placing a piece with only corners touching the existing pieces
  • No piece may overlap
